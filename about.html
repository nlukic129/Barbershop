WHO ARE WE

This Belgrade barbershop offers the full treatment with every cut (hot towel, straight razor shave), making it something of a place to hang out and chat as much as it is to clean up your look. Whether razor wispy cuts, edgy faded cuts, long layered styles, and sharp lined-up styles, high taper, flattop or rockabilly quiff. We recognize your time is valuable, so you are not required to wait at the shop if you have other things to accomplish during the day. We will call or text you 10-15 minutes prior to your turn so that you can be back to The Shave when your barber or stylist is ready for you.
